The Resort

A courtyard retreat,
built for slow evenings.

An echo of nature. A nest of calm.

Tucked off Rajawaram Road in the quiet countryside of Chinnapendyala, Hanamkonda, Mayukha EchoNest is built around traditional Telangana courtyard architecture — terracotta-tiled roofs, wooden pillars, and open verandas — set against acres of open lawn and palm groves.

By day, the grounds are sunlit and still. By evening, string lights trace the roofline and the courtyard glows gold — a setting equally suited to a quiet family outing, a celebration under the pergola, or an evening that simply asks for nothing at all.

Spaces & Experiences

What awaits you here

Sprawling Open Lawns

Acres of manicured green, ready for gatherings, games, and long conversations under the sky.

Traditional Dining Hall

Multi-cuisine dining beneath a warm chandelier glow, framed by red-brick courtyard walls.

Illuminated Pergola

A fairy-lit, pillared pavilion made for celebrations, sundowners, and golden-hour photographs.

Veranda Lounge Seating

Wicker chairs beneath lantern light — the spot for a quiet coffee or a late-night chat.

Landscaped Palm Gardens

Palms and flowering shrubs line every walkway, framing the courtyard from every angle.

Ample On-site Parking

Generous parking space right on the grounds, for both quiet visits and larger events.
